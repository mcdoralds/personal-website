OMEN Rewards

intro

OMEN's Reward program is built on Versus System’s proprietary prizing platform SDK. It is an engagement and prizing program that allows any PC player to play and win challenges based on a variety of in-game activities. Video game publishers and development studios are able to offer real-money and prize based challenges featuring their games and products, and brands are provided a unique opportunity to connect with a growing market in a more direct way.


Multi-language support available in the US, UK, Mexico, China, and India.


At Versus Systems, we collaborated with HP’s cross-functional teams, brand partners, game studios, agencies, partners, influencers, and the PC gaming community to deliver exciting prizes and integrated in-game experiences.
